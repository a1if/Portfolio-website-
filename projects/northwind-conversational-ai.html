Case Study · Conversational AI

Northwind Conversational AI

Launching a multilingual virtual banker trusted by regulated markets. I steered LLM adaptation, safety review, and performance analytics to deliver empathetic service across 5 languages while protecting user privacy.

Role
Applied Scientist
Timeline
8 months · 2021
Impact
56% increase in self-service resolution

Goals & success criteria

Elevate digital support without compromising compliance.

Customer goals

  • Automate tier-one banking inquiries across channels.
  • Maintain a consistent tone aligned with brand voice.
  • Meet regulatory obligations for data retention and audit logs.

Measured outcomes

  • Achieved 94% post-interaction CSAT across cohorts.
  • Reduced contact center volume by 42% within two quarters.
  • Cleared security, privacy, and bias assessments with zero major findings.

Solution approach

Combining frontier models with rigorous guardrails.

Modeling

  • Fine-tuned encoder-decoder LLMs using a curated corpus of call transcripts and intent taxonomies.
  • Implemented reinforcement learning from human feedback to align tone and policy compliance.
  • Built domain adapters to switch financial terminology based on locale and regulatory jurisdiction.

Safety & compliance

  • Developed toxicity, hallucination, and PII classifiers in the moderation stack.
  • Delivered red-teaming playbooks with 200+ adversarial prompts for quarterly testing.
  • Instrumented observability across latency, accuracy, and fairness KPIs via Datadog dashboards.

Leadership highlights

Shipping cross-functional workstreams with clarity.

Partnership

  • Partnered with design to script inclusive conversation flows and accessibility cues.
  • Coached product marketing on communicating AI capabilities and limitations honestly.
  • Coordinated with legal on explainability documentation for regulators.

Enablement

  • Built a labeling portal enabling 50 subject-matter experts to contribute RLHF comparisons.
  • Ran enablement sessions for customer success teams covering AI escalation workflows.
  • Established a weekly metrics review to triage drift, bias, and data quality signals.
