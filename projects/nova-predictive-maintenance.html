Case Study · Predictive Maintenance

Nova Predictive Maintenance

Delivering always-on reliability analytics for Nova Labs' industrial IoT fleet. I led the end-to-end ML platform workstream that paired graph neural networks with streaming telemetry to surface imminent equipment failures two weeks in advance.

Role
Lead Machine Learning Engineer
Timeline
10 months · 2022
Impact
$4.5M annual downtime savings

Objectives & outcomes

Modernize Nova's maintenance playbook with trustworthy AI.

Business goals

  • Reduce unplanned downtime across a 3,000 asset fleet.
  • Provide plant operators with actionable maintenance windows.
  • Standardize reporting for leadership across geographies.

Results

  • Forecasted 87% of critical failures at least 14 days early.
  • Improved technician scheduling efficiency by 22%.
  • Enabled finance to project parts inventory with 95% accuracy.

Technical architecture

Robust, observable infrastructure supporting rapid iteration and low-latency inference.

Data & modeling

  • Designed a feature store ingesting 40M sensor readings daily through Kafka and dbt.
  • Built graph neural networks capturing equipment dependencies and learned temporal attention weights.
  • Automated hyper-parameter sweeps with MLflow and Ray Tune, cutting experimentation time by 35%.

Operations

  • Containerized inference services on Kubernetes with autoscaling tuned for bursty telemetry loads.
  • Implemented drift monitoring, canary deployments, and rollback playbooks with Prometheus and Argo Rollouts.
  • Delivered Grafana dashboards surfacing confidence intervals and recommended maintenance actions per facility.

What I owned

Leadership spanning product discovery through production launch.

Discovery

  • Conducted ride-alongs with maintenance crews to map workflows.
  • Co-created leading indicators and economic impact models with operations finance.
  • Facilitated risk reviews focused on model transparency and user trust.

Delivery

  • Mentored a cross-functional squad of 6 engineers and 2 analysts.
  • Shipped the production stack, observability, and on-call runbooks.
  • Presented results to the board, securing budget for expansion to additional plants.
